RIP POP SMOKE!

Who is Pop Smoke ?

Bashar Barakah Jackson known Profesionally as Pop Smoke, was an American rapper born and raised in Canarise, Brooklyn.

First album of Pop Smoke named "Meet The Woo" with hits like Dior or Welcome To The Party

Second album of Pop Smoke named "Meet The Woo vol.2" with hits like Shake the room or Get Back

Album "Shoot for the Stars Aim For the Moon" realesed after Pop Smoke death

DEATH

Pop Smoke died on February 19, 2020, after being shot twice during a home invasion.On February 21, the Los Angeles County Department of Medical Examiner-Coroner revealed that the cause of Pop Smoke's death was a gunshot wound to the torso
